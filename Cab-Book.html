How to book a cab from Ola App -

Follow these steps -

  1. Launch Ola application.
  2. Provide location access.
  3. Tap on the Pickup location option.
  4. Enter the pick-up location.
  5. Tap on Drop location option.
  6. Enter Drop location.
  7. Select the type of ride.
  8. Tap on Ride Now.
  9. Confirm and book Ola.
