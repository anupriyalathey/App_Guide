How to Recharge a DTH -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. Go to 'Recharge & Pay Bills' Section
  3. Click on ‘DTH Recharge’
  4. Choose your DTH Operator
  5. Enter Registered Mobile No. / Subscriber ID
  6. Enter the Amount you want to Recharge
  7. Click on 'Proceed to Recharge' Button
  8. Choose the method to pay and click pay button
