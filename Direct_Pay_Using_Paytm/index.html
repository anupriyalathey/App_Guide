How to do mobile number-

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. Click on ‘To Mobile’ inside 'Send Money' tab
  3. Enter Mobile Number or You can also pick the mobile number from contacts saved on your device
  4. Enter the amount you want to pay
  5. Click on Proceed/Pay Button
  6. Choose the method to pay and click pay button
