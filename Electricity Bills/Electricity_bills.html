How to Make Electricity Bill Payment Online?

You can easily pay your water bill online on Paytm by following a few simple steps. The whole procedure takes only a minute of your time. Follow the steps below to make a water bill payment on Paytm-

On the Application-

  • Open Paytm App on your mobile 


  • Click on ‘Recharge and Pay Bills’

  • Click on ‘Electricity Bills’

  • Select your Electricity supply board



  • Select your service type (if any)




  • Enter the ‘customer identification number’ (such as Consumer Number, Connection Number, K Number etc.)

  • Click on ‘Proceed’ to check the water bill amount




  • Select your preferred mode of payment and complete the payment
