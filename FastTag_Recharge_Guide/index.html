How to do FastTag Recharge

Follow these Following steps :-

Step 1. Open the Paytm Mobile Application and click on the search Button.


Step 2. Type "Fastag" in the search button and click on "FasTag Recharge" Option.


Step 3. Select the Bank linked with FasTag.


Step 4. Enter the Vehicle Registration Number and click on "Proceed".


Step 5. Enter the amount and click on "Proceed".


Step 6. Select the Payment Method and click on "Pay Now".


Step 7. Congrats..Your FasTag has been Recharged.
