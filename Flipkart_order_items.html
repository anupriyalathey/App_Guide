How to buy a item from Flipkart -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Flipkart application
  2. Click on ‘Mobile section’
  3. Chose A Mobile
  4. Click on 'Buy Now'
  5. Enter Address
  6. Chose Payment
  7. Click on Confirm Order
  8. Your order is created
