How to Check PNR Status Of Booked Ticket -

Using Paytm

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. scroll down to the Ticket Booking section
  3. Click on Train Tickets
  4. Click on Check PNR
  5. Enter the 10 Digits PNR Number
  6. After Entering PNR Number click on Check Now
