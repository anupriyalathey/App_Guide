How to Reset Forgotten Password in PAYTM -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. Tap on the three lines
  3. click on the 'security settings' option
  4. click on the 'change password' option
  5. click on 'Forgot Password'
  6. click on 'OK' button
  7. Open your Phone Dialer
  8. Enter the paytm customer care No.(0120-488-8488 or 0120-4456-456)

  12. Tap on paytm message
  13. Click on the link
  14. Enter your 'New Paytm Password'
  15. Re-Enter the 'Password' for confirmation
  16. Tap on Proceed'
