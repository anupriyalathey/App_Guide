How to Book Ticket Using IRCTC -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the IRCTC mobile application
  2. Click on Plan My Journey
  3. Enter Details for the Journey
  4. Click on Search Trains
  5. Select the Most Suitable Train as according to the availability
  6. Add thePassenger
  7. Put the Details of each
  8. Fill out all the options in the screen Address and all
  9. Check the summary and Enter the Captcha click Proceed to Pay
  10. Select the Payment Options
  11. Click the Proceed Button
