How to Book a Gas Cylinder -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. Go to 'Recharge & Pay Bills' Section
  3. Click on ‘Book Gas Cylinder’
  4. Be sure, you choose a 'book a cylinder' option
  5. Select your gas provider
  6. Select 'Booking type value'
  7. Enter your 'gas connection details' like consumer No./mobile No./LPG ID
  8. Click on Proceed to Book Cylinder
