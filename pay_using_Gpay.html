How to setup Google Pay -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Google Pay mobile application
  2. Click on ‘New Payment’
  3. Click on ‘Select Payment option e.g - Bank transfer, Phone number, UPI id.’
  4. Enter Details
  5. Click on ‘Pay’
  6. Enter Amount
  7. Click on ‘Next’
  8. Enter Passcode
  9. Your Payment is done. Take screenshot.
